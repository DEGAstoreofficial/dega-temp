Willows: A Dream of Shadows

A tale of two siblings struggling to survive their present and heal from their past. Just how long can they ignore that which haunts them? "Willows: A Dream of Shadows" is a psychological gothic horror visual novel puzzle game, featuring hand drawn assets, and a episodic branching story-line.

Demo available

About the game

"Matthew Willows, I know you well enough to know you’ve never been 'fine' in your life." - Maria Willows

The Willows siblings have learned that it’s best to keep their heads down and go with the flow of life. At least that’s how the “responsible” older sister, Maria, sees it, as she contends with a life of managing a run-down video store in a run-down city, in a run-down country.

Her little brother, Matthew, sometimes forgets the lessons life has taught; preoccupying himself with those annoying questions of “who are we” and “where do we come from”. Matthew, with his perpetual curiosity, just wants to get to the bottom of it all. But Maria, in all her wisdom and experience, knows that the only thing worth getting to the bottom of is a bottle of beer after a long day's work.

Matthew knows things, too. Matthew sees things. He sees the Shadows.

The Shadows are always waiting, always watching.

Features

  • A branching, multi-part story where your choices matter
  • Multiple protagonists with real-time swapping
  • Lots of hand-drawn artwork in a variety of styles
  • A unique and original dynamic soundtrack
  • Explorable maps filled with puzzles and challenges
